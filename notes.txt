  -----LangChain-----------------------------------------
  - To build RAG you need to:
1. Read your CSV
2. Cut it into small pieces
3. Send pieces to OpenAI to convert to numbers
4. Store those numbers
5. Later — search those numbers
6. Send results to OpenAI to get an answer

LangChain = pre-written code for ALL 6 of these steps
            so you don't write it from scratch


Large Language Model (LLM)
→ The AI brain that thinks and answers
→ Examples: GPT-4, Claude, Gemini
→ Made by: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google
→ It UNDERSTANDS and GENERATES text

LangChain
→ Has NO brain of its own
→ Cannot think or answer anything
→ It is just a CONNECTOR tool
→ Like a pipe that connects your data to the LLM brain

LangChain = toolkit/connector (no brain)
GPT-4     = the actual LLM brain (does the thinking)

LangChain uses GPT-4 to do the thinking.
LangChain itself cannot think at all.


Normal database search:
"Find rows containing the word Python"
→ Only finds EXACT word matches

ChromaDB search:
"Find rows similar in MEANING to my question"
→ Finds rows about programming languages
→ Even if they say 'coding' not 'Python'
→ Because similar meaning = similar numbers

--------openai uses----------
Tool 1 — Embedding Model
Name:    text-embedding-3-small
Job:     converts text → numbers
Used:    twice in RAG pipeline
         → once for your CSV chunks
         → once for your question
Cost:    very cheap (fraction of a cent)

Tool 2 — Chat Model (LLM/GPT)
Name:    gpt-4o
Job:     reads text → writes intelligent answer
Used:    once at the very end only
Cost:    slightly more expensive


text-embedding-3-small   →   converts your 61,951 jobs to numbers
                             converts your question to numbers

gpt-4o                   →   reads the 5 relevant jobs found
                             writes a smart answer for you

✅ LangChain          splits your data into chunks
✅ text-embedding-3-small  converts chunks to numbers
✅ ChromaDB           stores + searches numbers
✅ text-embedding-3-small  converts your question to numbers
✅ ChromaDB           finds most relevant chunks
✅ gpt-4o             reads chunks + writes answer
